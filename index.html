Materials & Design Politecnico di Torino · 2025

Material Selection: Motorcycle Front Fork Assembly

Systematic material selection for a motorcycle front fork inner tube using ANSYS Granta EduPack. The study combined performance index optimization with lifecycle sustainability analysis.

  • 4 performance indexes derived from first principles (stiffness, fracture toughness, fatigue life, weight)
  • Ashby diagram analysis filtering 4181 materials down to AISI 1095 carbon steel
  • Multi-criteria decision matrix: supply chain, manufacturability, recyclability
  • Eco-Audit LCA: CO₂ footprint comparison across transport scenarios (truck vs rail vs air)
